About Serving a Mission

Serving a mission for The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints is a profound opportunity to share faith, grow spiritually, and serve others selflessly. It allows individuals to dedicate their time and talents to spreading the gospel to every corner of the earth and helping communities around the world.

Reasons to Serve:

  1. Spiritual Growth
    • Builds your understanding of the gospel
    • Practice baring testimony of what you know to be true
    • Time to learn for yourself
    • Practice the attribute of Jesus Christ
  2. Service to Others
    • Become a "fisher of Men"
    • Share the light of Christ
    • Create life long friendships
  3. Personal Development
    • Learn another language
    • Emerse yourself in a different culture
    • Living on yourown
    • Learn to cook and clean for yourself

I learned that being diligent is essential to success in this life in order to help others come unto Christ, but also to succeed in a professional career. This attribute of Christ is something that we all need in order to progress here on earth. I also learned a great deal about my testimony of the restoration of the gospel.
